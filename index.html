Mission

Modeling Spatial Intelligence Through Structured Representations

Thinking Shapes Lab explores how to represent real-world spaces as intelligent abstractions. Our work connects computer vision, artificial intelligence, and generative modeling to build systems that perceive, abstract, and recreate the physical world - distilling the complexity of space into compact, meaningful, and interactive representations.

Research Directions

Open-World 3D Scene Understanding

Class-agnostic and open-vocabulary segmentation of point clouds and 3D scenes.

Compact 3D Scene Representations

Compact, interpretable 3D scene representations using geometric primitives.

Functional & Interactive 3D Scenes

Affordance and functionality reasoning in indoor environments for robotic interaction.
