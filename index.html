Mission

Modeling Spatial Intelligence Through Shape

Thinking Shapes Lab explores how three-dimensional scenes become intelligent abstractions. Our work connects computer vision, artificial intelligence, and generative modeling to build systems that perceive, abstract, and recreate the physical world — distilling the complexity of space into compact, meaningful, and interactive representations.

Research Directions

3D Instance & Semantic Segmentation

Class-agnostic and open-vocabulary segmentation of point clouds and 3D scenes at scale.

Superquadric Scene Decomposition

Compact, interpretable 3D scene representations using superquadric geometric primitives.

Functional 3D Understanding

Affordance and functionality reasoning in indoor environments for robotic interaction.
