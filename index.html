Mission

Modeling Thought Through Shape

Thinking Shapes Lab explores how geometric structures become cognitive structures. Our work connects computational design, machine intelligence, and visual reasoning to build systems that understand and generate meaningful form.

Research Directions

3D Instance & Semantic Segmentation

Class-agnostic and open-vocabulary segmentation of point clouds and 3D scenes at scale.

Superquadric Scene Decomposition

Compact, interpretable 3D scene representations using superquadric geometric primitives.

Functional 3D Understanding

Affordance and functionality reasoning in indoor environments for robotic interaction.
