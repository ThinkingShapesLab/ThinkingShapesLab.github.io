Mission

Modeling Spatial Intelligence Through Compact Representations

Thinking Shapes Lab explores how three-dimensional scenes become intelligent abstractions. Our work connects computer vision, artificial intelligence, and generative modeling to build systems that perceive, abstract, and recreate the physical world — distilling the complexity of space into compact, meaningful, and interactive representations.
